UDL Element Quick Reference

Based on CAST UDL Guidelines 3.0, effective design provides multiple means of:

  • Engagement: The "Why" of learning. Includes optimizing choice, relevance, and authenticity[cite: 70].
  • Representation: The "What" of learning. Includes clarifying vocabulary and using multiple media[cite: 71].
  • Action & Expression: The "How" of learning. Includes using assistive technologies and multiple tools for construction[cite: 71, 72].
