The ARC Coaching Protocol

Acknowledge Validate the teacher's current reality or frustration without judgment[cite: 24, 26]. Focus on the barrier, not a student deficit.
Reframe Shift the perspective toward UDL principles[cite: 27]. Separate the instructional goal from the means of achieving it[cite: 37, 72].
Commit Agree on a small, actionable next step to test a flexible strategy in the classroom[cite: 29, 37].
