The Notice-Wonder-Next Protocol

Notice Observe specific evidence or data. "I notice that when we use text-only slides, certain students disengage."
Wonder Pose an inquiry based on UDL goals. "I wonder what would happen if we provided a visual scaffold or choice in how they respond?"
Next Determine the immediate small step. "Next time, let's trial a graphic organizer or a 'Plus One' visual."
