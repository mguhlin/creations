Before You Post: Staff Checklist
  • ☐ Confirm student has given written consent for this specific post.
  • ☐ Verify the safe version is accurate (no hallucinated replacements).
  • ☐ Check that no combination of details can narrow to one student.
  • ☐ Confirm award amount visibility matches your disclosure policy.
  • ☐ Obtain supervisor or communications lead approval before publishing.
  • ☐ Save approved version to your social media content log.

We Will

  • Use first name + field of study to celebrate students.
  • Share generalized details (city region, major category).
  • Obtain written consent before using any student content.
  • Run all posts through a privacy check before publishing.

We Will Not

  • Combine full name, school, location, and award in one post.
  • Tag or @mention students without explicit consent.
  • Disclose award amounts without student/policy approval.
  • Publish before a staff reviewer approves the final copy.
