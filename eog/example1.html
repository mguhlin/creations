Safety & Compliance

All team members are required to complete safety and compliance training before independently performing job duties. The following must be finished during your first week unless otherwise noted.

LMS

KPA Training Platform

KPA is our Learning Management System for OSHA safety modules, hazard communication (HazCom), and HR compliance training. Your manager will assign your initial course list. Log in at hcm.kpa.io.

Required — Week 1

Initial Safety Certifications

Complete all assigned KPA modules within your first 5 business days. Includes: General Safety Orientation, Chemical / HazMat Handling, and Emergency Procedures for your department.

Ongoing

Annual Re-Certifications

Certain certifications expire annually. KPA will send automated reminders. It is your responsibility to complete renewals before the expiration date noted in your profile.

Policy

Workplace Conduct

Review the Mac Haik Employee Handbook provided by HR. Key policies include: anti-harassment, social media use, vehicle test drive authorization, and lot safety protocols.

Non-negotiable: No employee may perform any lift, chemical handling, or vehicle movement task without completing the relevant KPA safety module first. Supervisors are responsible for verifying completion.

First Week Checklist

Use this checklist to track your onboarding milestones. Your department manager should sign off on each completed item.

  • Verify employment eligibility (I-9) and submit required documentation to HR
  • Receive company email credentials and change temporary password
  • Log in to the Mac Haik Employee Portal and verify personal information
  • Set up direct deposit and confirm W-4 withholding
  • Complete Benefits Enrollment (deadline: Day 10)
  • Receive CDK Global login and complete role-based orientation module
  • Activate Microsoft Teams on desktop and mobile
  • Complete TrueSpot vehicle tracking orientation with lot manager
  • Receive KPA login and complete all assigned Week 1 safety modules
  • Obtain manufacturer portal login (Ford / CDJR — if applicable)
  • Review Mac Haik Employee Handbook and sign acknowledgment form
  • Complete lot safety walkthrough with department supervisor
  • Shadow a senior team member for your role for a minimum of [hours — fill in]
  • Submit completed onboarding paperwork to HR before end of Week 1
Reminder: Some system access requires manager approval. If you have not received credentials for a system by Day 3, notify your manager immediately — do not wait until the end of the week.
